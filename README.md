### 1. Objective

Score the most **Bloom Points** by successfully growing and harvesting plants within 15 minutes.

### 2. Player Roles & Setup

* **Players:** 2‚Äì4 players.
* **Starting Kit:** Each player starts with 3 empty pots, 1 watering can (unlimited water), and 50 "Seed Credits."
* **The Shop:** A shared central market that refreshes every 60 seconds with random seeds and fertilizers.

### 3. Core Gameplay Loop

Players must manage three main variables for every plant:

* **Hydration:** Plants lose water over time. If the meter hits zero, the plant wilts and stops growing.
* **Sunlight:** Players must drag their pots to different windows (North, South, East, West) as the "sun" moves across the screen during the 15-minute round.
* **Pests:** Occasionally, "Aphids" will appear on a plant. Players must click them away or use a "Ladybug Power-up."
